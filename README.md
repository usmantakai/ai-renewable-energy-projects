## Overview
This repository contains projects and experiments focused on applying **Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence** to **renewable energy systems**, smart grids, and energy optimization problems.

The work here combines **engineering fundamentals** with **data-driven modeling** to address real-world energy challenges, particularly in developing and emerging power systems.

## Background
I am a **Mechanical Engineering graduate** with strong interests in:
- Renewable Energy Systems
- Energy Forecasting & Optimization
- Smart Grids
- AI/ML applications in energy

My research and practical focus is on using **Python-based ML tools** to improve energy efficiency, reliability, and sustainability.

## Project Focus Areas
Projects in this repository may include:
- Renewable energy generation forecasting (solar, hybrid systems)
- Load demand prediction using ML models
- Energy data analysis and visualization
- AI-driven optimization for microgrids and smart grids
- Experimental notebooks and simulations

Each project is documented with clear objectives, datasets (where applicable), and results.
